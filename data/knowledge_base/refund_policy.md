# Refund 

Customers on monthly plans may request a refund within 14 calendar days of the original payment date if they have not exceeded standard usage thresholds.

Annual plans are eligible for a partial refund only within 7 days of payment. After 7 days, annual plans are not refundable.

If custom onboarding has already been delivered or completed, refunds are not available regardless of billing cycle.

Refund requests must be submitted through the billing support team via the dashboard.

Refund processing typically takes between 5 and 7 business days after approval.
