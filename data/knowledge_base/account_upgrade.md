# Account Upgrade Policy

Customers can upgrade their plan at any time through the billing settings page.

Upgrades take effect immediately once payment is processed.

For monthly subscriptions, charges are prorated based on the remaining time in the billing cycle.

For annual plans, additional seats or feature upgrades are billed proportionally for the remaining contract term.

After upgrading, newly added seats may take up to 10 minutes to appear in the workspace.

Downgrades are not applied immediately and will take effect at the next billing cycle.
